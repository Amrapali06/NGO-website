Standing Up For Justice And Equality

Building Bridges Of Hope And Compassion

An institution for imparting dignified education to poor, oppressed, underprivileged students of 'all castes and religions. Inspired by the educational and social movement of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jyotirao Phule, Savitrimai Phule, Shahu Maharaj and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, We started a movement to provide financial and psychological support to the students of oppressed and underprivileged communities and for their overall progress. For this we are making an NGO named 'Saving a Future through Empowerment' 'SAFE Wings' which will mainly work on the subject of 'Education'. And by increasing its scope in the future, NGO will be able to do this work on many subjects like Health, Women Empowerment, Sports.

Campaign Achieved...

Safe Wings honors Shiv Jayanti with an essay contest on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's teachings, fostering values among students while commemorating his legacy.

On October 1, 2023, Safe Wings held a meditation workshop for Sant Kabir Nagar students, led by Prabuddha sir. Weekly workshops teach new skills, supported by contributors and volunteers.

Safe Wings visited educationally adopted students to assess their academic progress. They checked notebooks individually and asked questions to evaluate their understanding and development.

Safe Wings conducted academic assessments for adopted students, reviewing their progress through individual notebook checks and interactive questioning sessions.

Safe Wings hosted a Shiv Jayanti essay contest, inspiring students to ponder Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's teachings. Honored winners reflect Safe Wings' commitment to noble values.

Celebrating Republic Day, Safe Wings distributed school supplies to 41 children in Sant Kabir Nagar, embodying unity and empowerment. Grateful for Safe Wings' contribution.
